Welcome to transformato’s documentation!

transformato is a lightweight implementation of the Boresch-Bruckner Serial-Atom-Insertion1 Common-Core (SAI-CC) approach2 to relative binding/solvation free energy calculations3. It does so by connecting the physical endstates of two molecules via two separate alchemical paths.

It requires very little set-up time and is designed to work directly with output from CHARMM - GUI, making it a quick and simple way to run accurate RBFE/RSFE calculations in either CHARMM or openMM.

If you’d like to take a look at the code, head over to our github repository.
