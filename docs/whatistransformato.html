Acknowledgements

Apart from the default python packages, transformato relies in large parts on the following packages:

  • MDAnalysis1 2 : Used for general purpose protein and ligand analysis, atom selection and especially trajectory analysis.

  • Numpy3 : Well, its numpy. We use it for math.

  • parmed4 : Protein structure manipulations.

  • RDKit: Visualisation of molecular structures.
